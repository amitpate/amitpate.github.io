About the founder

For over a decade I've built companies where artificial intelligence meets human imagination — across sport, film and interactive entertainment.

Generative AIText-to-3DAI Game Generation Virtual ProductionComputer Vision0→1 ProductVenture Building

I work at the intersection of technology and storytelling — pairing a background in engineering with training in film. That combination is the throughline of everything I build: using AI to erase the distance between an idea and a finished creative work.

With Snaptivity I put AI-driven robotic cameras into stadiums — work that won two Gold Lions at Cannes. With PentoPix I built an AI platform that turns scripts into cinematic 3D animation, earning SXSW Pitch's Best in Show and a place in TechCrunch's Startup Battlefield 200. Today I'm building Orio — generate playable 3D games from plain words — and Tw!st, a football prediction game reinventing how fans live the match.

I'm recognised among the entrepreneurs shaping generative AI for the creative, media and entertainment industries — and I'm only getting started.
